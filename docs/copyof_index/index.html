I am Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Economic Studies (IES) of Charles University in Prague. I am primarily interested in the fields of Macroeconomics and Monetary Economics. My research focuses on exploring the impact of expectations and uncertainty on household decision-making, as well as their implications for inequality and the overall economy.
